Summary
Not your typical creative writer or content creator. Of course, I have strong writing skills, geek out over cliffhangers, and proofread my own social media captions. But I’m also a graphic curator, community builder, team leader, and a committed worker. I am confident in my ability to come up with interesting ideas for unforgettable marketing campaigns. I’m not the creator who just wants to write or curate. I’m the creator who actually wants to promote, write, plan, ideate, and lead. This is what I do best and love most.
